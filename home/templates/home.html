The greatest story never told... The African Redemptive Struggle Museum An Igala Kingdom USA Enterprise The ARS museum once, known as the Gullah Heritage Museum, is America’s first anti-slavery resistance museum dedicated to the African-American quest for freedom from 1526 to 1858. It houses the largest selection of anti-slavery resistance art in America. ARS Museum is 100% owned and operated by descendants of “self liberated Africans” (a.k.a. maroons) and house in the historic Independent African Methodist Episcopal Church founded in 1898. Come be inspired by the masterful paintings of the people and events that shaped early American history
